Designed to grow merchants with us — not reward turnover alone
TierScore bandTenure floorService floorMax ticketRateCollateralWhat unlocks

Design intent: A merchant who only uses our QR for two months and asks for ₹10 L gets Bronze. A 4-year member using SB+FD+Gold+UPI and attending AGMs gets Gold — even if turnover is modest. The score punishes "loan-and-leave" behaviour and rewards staying.
