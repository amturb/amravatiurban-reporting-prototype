Top three financial risks the board should debate

auditor lens
  1. SLR-equivalent at 15.3% — sub-statutory liquidity. Need to park ≥ ₹85 L more in approved securities / FD; currently ₹1.05 Cr in one bank (Jijau) = concentration risk too.
  2. CRAR ~3.2% — well below the 9% prudential floor. Either raise ₹50–60 L more share capital (authorised cap ₹2 Cr leaves room) or slow advance growth.
  3. DRS book 83% overdue (₹12.63 L on ₹15.17 L) — single product driving Gross NPA from 5.4% → 8.1%. Either recover or fully provide and write off; current 22% provision coverage is light.
